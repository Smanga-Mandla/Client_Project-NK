About NK Shuttles

NK Shuttles (Pty) Ltd is a professional shuttle service company established in 2019 by its founder, Mr Karel Sibuyani. The company was founded by a youthful and dynamic entrepreneur with strong industry experience gained through working within the shuttle and transport sector. Over the past five years, NK Shuttles has grown steadily by expanding its service offerings and operational footprint across South Africa. Our commitment to reliability, safety, and professionalism has enabled us to build a trusted reputation within the travel and transport industry. Our dedicated team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to ensure efficient and seamless service delivery.
